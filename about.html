About Us

BiyaheTayo is a travel companion app designed to help local and foreign tourists explore to rich culture, heritage, and hidden gems of the Philippines-starting with Tarlac Province. Our goal is to break language barriers and enhance tourism experience through smart technology. BiyaheTayo empowers users to travel confidently and connect with local communities.
